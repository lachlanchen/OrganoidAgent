# OrganoIDNetData (Zenodo 10643410)

## Purpose
- Curated cell-life imaging dataset of immune-enriched pancreatic cancer organoids for AI segmentation and benchmarking.

## Research Topic
- Organoid morphology and immune co-culture imaging in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (human and murine PDAC).

## Paper
- Kulkarni A., Ferreira N., Scodellaro R., et al. "A Curated Cell Life Imaging Dataset of Immune-enriched Pancreatic Cancer Organoids with Pre-trained AI Models." *Scientific Data* 11, 820 (2024).
- https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-024-03631-3
